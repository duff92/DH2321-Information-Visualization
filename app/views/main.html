This is a project in the course Information Visualization (DH2321) at KTH. The graph below illustrates a comparision between some countries regarding how subjective gradings of health and happiness (collected from World Values Survey) could be compared with data of spending on health products in each country (collected from Gapminder).
{{series.name}}

Description of variables from World Values Survey

  • Feeling of happiness: 1 (Not at all happy) to 4 (Very happy)
  • State of health(subjective): 1(Poor) to 4(Very good)
  • Satisfaction with your life: 1(Completely dissatisfied) to 10 (Completely satisfied)
  • Science and technology (health): 1 (Completely disagee) to 10 (Completely agree)
